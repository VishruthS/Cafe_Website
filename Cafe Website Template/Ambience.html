Cafe is not just about good food; it requires great ambience and a lot more. It is very important to have extraordinary ambience for which the interior decoration should be apt. It is important to have cozy interior in a café as it affects the minds of the customers. People like visiting places which give them freshness.

Virtual Impact

The interior decoration of a café represents it and creates a visual impact on the customer. It becomes a part of the café’s identity. The ambience creates a virtual impact on the customer’s mind which makes them want to visit frequently. Keeping this in mind we have decorated our cafe . We have a variety of seating arrangements so that it looks more attractive and also gives more comfort.


Affects Mood

The interior decoration will help decide the customer’s mood. Interior decoration is also a work of art, placing furniture at specific places, different arrangements, lighting, décor, the wall colours everything has a feel to it. The interior helps in changing the perception towards food and ambience. So we have set up our cafe in a unique fashion to comfort our customers and to give them the best expierence. And also we will decorate our cafe for you and bring a smile on your face by helping you in surprising your special ones.


Pets and Games

Chikoo's cafe is the only pet cafe in Mysuru. We have tried to make our cafe more interesting by keeping some board games for our customers. We assure you that you won't feel bored in our cafe as we have games and some amazingly cute pets. Pets are mood changers for sure, all the custormes are free to play with our cute puppies and cats.
