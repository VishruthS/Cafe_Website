Who are We?


MCC Chikoo’s Cafe is a premier cafe in Mysore that is into Food catering and supply services.

Our core emphasis is on food quality and healthy home-made food. We cook a variety of local and authentic dishes from native cuisines across Karnataka with a blend of authentic spices in a clean environment.


What We Serve?



Quality ingredients, prepared by people who care: that’s what we do. Questions about our products? We’re always happy to chat.

Whether you’re starting the day, need an afternoon break, or just really need a cup of tea, let us serve you — it’s what we do best.
